What is this domain?

{{ apex_host }} is the secure device-access domain for ReSuture's NAVISLab surgical training simulators. Each simulator is reachable only through an encrypted tunnel, gated by Cloudflare Access: customers authenticate with a one-time PIN sent to their registered email address.

No credentials are collected or stored on this domain. If you arrived here unexpectedly, you most likely want resuture.com, or your simulator's sign-in at {{ portal_host }}.
